07

Voice & Tone

✓ Do
  • → "Enterprise expertise, SMB agility"
  • → Confident, direct, outcome-focused
  • → Use active voice and concrete benefits
  • → Lead with the business value, then the how
✗ Don't
  • → Avoid jargon without explanation
  • → Never sound overly casual or flippant
  • → Avoid vague claims ("best-in-class")
  • → Don't lead with features — lead with outcomes
